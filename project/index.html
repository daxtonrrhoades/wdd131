Star Wars Summary

Prequels

Two Jedi Knights, Qui Gon Jinn, and Obi Wan Kenobi, are sent to negotiate with the Trade Federation.

While there they are attacked by Separatist droids and have to battle their way out.

They end up making their way to Tattooine and discover a young junnkyard slave, Anakin Skywalker, who they believe could be the Chosen One.

Qui Gon Jinn is killed by Darth Maul and Obi Wan must train the young boy.

In the next film, 10 years later, Anakin is a grown Jedi Knight and training under Obi Wan as his padawan.

The Jedi obtain a clone army who are clones of a bounty hunter named Jango Fett. The Separatists make moves and begin the Clone Wars.

In the third and final film of the prequels, Anakin forms a friendly bond with Chancellor Palpatine.

When Anakin is asked to spy on the Chancellor, he is filled with anger and is drawn to the dark side.

Obi Wan and Anakin battle on the lava planet of Mustafar where Padme follows and has birth to twins shortly before passing away.

Obi Wan defeats Anakin but does not finish him off. Palpatine rescues Anakin and creates Darth Vader.

Original Trilogy

A farmboy named Luke Skywalker is sent by Old Ben, also known as Obi Wan Kenobi, to rescu princess Leia who has been taken hostage by Darth Vader.

He is accompanied by the Captain of the Millenium Falcon, Han Solo, and his fuzzy friend Chewbacca along with Old Ben and the droids, R2D2 and C3PO.

On the Death Star Obi Wan Sacrifices himself to get the others to their ship safely and stays behind with Vader and falls at his hand.

In the next film, we start on the snowy planet Hoth and Luke and his friends are attacked by Imperial forces commanded by Darth Vader.

Luke travels to Dagobah to learn the ways of the Jedi and meets Yoda, an old friend of Bens and a Jedi Master who will teach Luke.

Luke is influenced by the force to go help his friends and there he learns that Darth Vader is his father. Han is also frozen in Carbonite.

In the next film Luke is determined to bring his father back to the light side. He returns to Dagobah to finish his training and Yoda passes away.

Han is freed from Carbonite at Jabba's Palace and Luke is thrown into the Rancor's put to be eaten up. Luke is victorious and they escape the palace.

Luke is put up against Vader and his master Darth Sidious as Luke's friends plan to blow up the second Death Star

Luke convinces Vader there is still good in him and together they defeat Sidious. Though due to being electricuted Vader does not survive
