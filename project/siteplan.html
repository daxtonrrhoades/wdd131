Planning Site

Site Name: Wicketpedia

This name is a fun mix between Wikipedia and a famous Ewok named Wicket from Star Wars

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform the audience about the Star Wars Story and other information

Scenarios

  • Who was the most powerful Jedi?
  • Who was the most powerful Sith?
  • Where can I go to find more information about Star Wars?

Color Schema

I will have multiple colors, but some of them being Black(#07070A) and Mustard(#FFDD4A)

Black will be a primary color and will be used for the header and footer background.

Mustard will be used as a font Color

Typography

I will be using the Google fonts DynaPuff for headings and Exo 2 for paragraph and other text elements
